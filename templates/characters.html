Ryan: 17, m. An unsatisfied 17-year-old. Used to attend a private school in Brooklyn, now attends public school in the city and works for his mother’s restaurant in Chinatown. Kind of your basic Asian guy - not too bro-ish, not too artsy, but a little of both.

Vera: 17, f. His precocious best friend from SAT prep (since 7th grade, of course. We start early). Confident, but semi-closeted about her sexuality. Funny. Attends the academy (Windsor) that Ryan used to go to.

Patrick: 17, m. Ryan’s other best friend. Upbeat, a little dramatic, very hardworking. A big nerd. Also attends Windsor Academy.

Ms. Chen: 53, f. Ryan’s mother. A struggling restaurant owner and single mom. Worrisome, strong-willed, tired.

Michelle: 17, f. A non-Asian girl with a taste for Asian food. Cheerful, bright, and curious, but guarded. Craves approval.

Mrs. A: Vera’s mother. Very religious, proper, a bit snobby, traditional. “Helicopter mom.”

Mrs. B/Student 1: Patrick’s mother. Youthful in spirit, a little more “Americanized” than her peers. “Cool mom.”

Mrs. C/Student 2: Another friend of Ms. Chen’s and a mother of Windsor Academy students. “Normal mom.”

Everyman: Every adult man in the show, played by one person. (Hippie, Teacher, Boba guy, Suited man, Well-dressed gentleman, etc.)
